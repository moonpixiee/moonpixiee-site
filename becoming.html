Journal Prompts

Questions worth sitting with.

What version of yourself have you been performing? And what would it feel like to stop?

02

03

Shadow Work

The work nobody talks about.

Becoming isn't just about adding new habits. It's about facing the parts of you that were taught to hide — and choosing them anyway.

🌑

The Shadow

The parts of you that were rejected, suppressed, or deemed "too much." Shadow work is the practice of bringing them into the light without judgment.

🪞

The Mirror

What irritates you in others is often what you haven't accepted in yourself. This work starts with radical honesty about what you're actually seeing.

✨

Integration

The goal isn't to eliminate your shadow — it's to integrate it. Wholeness, not perfection. That's where the real magnetism lives.
